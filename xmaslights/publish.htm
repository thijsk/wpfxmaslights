Brokenwire.NET presents:

Christmas Lights

by Thijs Kroesbergen

This is a very small application specially build to make Christmas time a bit more enjoyable. Especially when you spend most of it behind your computer!

The Brokenwire.NET Christmas Lights application surrounds your desktop with several Christmas lights to bring you that holiday atmosphere!

  • Cool blinking lights on your desktop(s)
  • Non-obtrusive (you can still get work done!)
  • Easy to install!
  • FREE
